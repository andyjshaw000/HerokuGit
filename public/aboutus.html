Our Timeline

June 2021

This month was when we first started talking. We started going to the gym together and getting Menchie's afterwards. We also really enjoyed skating together. We'd also go to Round One a lot with the group.

July 2021

This month was when I started liking you. You introduced me to bouldering and I remember telling myself that I couldn't have these feelings for you. I also remember trying to step up my skating game to try and impress you. This was the month when we ran around Green Lake, walked the Seattle Pier, painted at Edmonds, went to UW/ Lake Union late at night, made s'mores, got Indian food, was my first time coming over to eat pho after Lake Blanca, and climbed Gas Works.

Beginning of August 2021

This has to be split into parts because this was a hectic month for us. This was when I would start coming over often to watch movies with you. Your birthday had happened, and I ended up staying at your house to play Cooking Mama and hung out with your siblings for the first time playing Catan.

August 9th, 2021

On this day we went bouldering together and got Fremont Bowl to eat at a park. Afterwards, we really wanted desert and you suggested we get Snowy Village. We ended up going back to your house that day to watch The Grudge, Curious George, and Howl's Moving Castle... Then I told you I liked you. It was scary and I remember the night before preparing what I would say to you. The next day, we met at Zumiez and you told me you had never thought of me like that. It had really hurt my feelings, but your concerns and feelings were valid and I knew that I would still want to be your friend. We went to Chic-Fil-A, Round the Table, and skated afterwards, but it was slightly awkward for me.

August 22nd, 2021

Despite feelings not being reciprocated, we still hung out. I went to Uyen's house to play Codenames with you guys after playing soccer, we got Cafe Rio after going to Richmond Beach and then watched Shrek 2 and did a puzzle, we even got Jack in the Box and pushed each other around in a shopping cart. Then, we went to the Airbnb with pp party. I remember you going out to look for me and feeling happy that you went out of your way to do that. I wasn't ready to have the conversation with you about us. However, this was when you told me you liked me back. I found out that you had feelings for me since July and that you asked for advice from your neighbor about what to do. Although we both had the same feelings for each other, it was difficult to decide what to do with those feelings. We settled on trying to work on ourselves and being very casual.

August 27th, 2021

Our nerves about our future together led to our Zumiez meet up once again to set ground rules and confirm our feelings. I remember feeling so ecstatic when I found out that you liked me back... and on the 27th, we began our "ooching". We got black bean noodles at Sahm Gook Jih and ate at Esperance Park, began telling people about us, had Menchie's, played We're Not Really Strangers, drew the scene of us at the park and watched Good Will Hunting. It was our first time doing something as a couple and not as friends.

September 3rd, 2021

This was a super cute day. This is when you told me you had a surprise for me and I met you at a park in Edmonds to eat Panda Express. This was also the day we went to Build-a-Bear to first order our Froggys! We went back to my place and built our Daiso dinosaur, made a peach cobbler, and watched Hereditary. This was also the day you tried hickory barbecue chips for the first time and my mac and cheese concoction with hot dogs.

September 19th, 2021

The birthday of our frog children.

October 7th, 2021

Things were amazing up to this point. We went to a band rally, walked around downtown Seattle late at night, painted at an art studio, had your salmon butter baked dish, and explored West Seattle. This was when I remember getting the text to meet you outside my house and we ended our "ooching" and went back to being friends. It wouldn't be until the 16th that we would see each other in person again and the 17th in Portland that I would tell you that I was okay with waiting for you to be ready for a relationship.

October 29th - November 1st, 2021

Although I wasn't sure what our status was, I knew that I at least wanted to be friends with you if you wanted to just stay as friends. The 29th was my birthday which was a blast going to a trampoline park together, exploring Tacoma tasting Ramen and playing at Chuck-e-Cheese. The 31st was Halloween and I broke down trying to act okay around everyone and this was when we sat down and had our talk about your decision. You said that you would decide the next day and after some talking and deliberation in the car outside the pp house, we ended up finally being an official relationship on the 1st of November.

Today

Ever since that day, things have been amazing. We have had some minor issues, but they're always resolved quickly because of how we communicate. We have had so many fun days and great dates and I am so excited for more to come. We have done so many fun things together like going to escape rooms, going to the Seattle Ferris Wheel, having sleep overs, snowboarding, playing Guilty Party, going to Discovery Park and Pike Place, having a drawing competition, playing Overcooked, celebrating your mom's birthday, and so much more. Despite all the hiccups, I wouldn't trade any of those experiences for the world. Thank you for everything, Trinh. I can't wait to see what our future is like!
